Kindergarten Curriculum

Core Elements of Our Program

Imaginative Learning

Free, imaginative play and purposeful work — kneading bread, sweeping, building, caring for the room. Through meaningful play, children learn to think, solve, and do.

Whole-Brain, Whole Child

The brain’s core architecture is wired in the early years. Our program builds rich connections across attention, memory, regulation, and language.

Music & the Arts

Singing, painting, modeling, handwork — color, song, and story are woven through every day. The arts build focus, fine motor skills, and a love of beauty.

Growing in Community

Reading others, working through conflict, steadying big feelings — children develop the social fluency and self-regulation that last a lifetime.

Nature & the Farm

Digging, planting, climbing, tending animals — outdoors in all weather. On the farm and beyond, children grow a rooted connection to the living world.

Wonder & Connection

Wonder, merriment, awe — the gifts of early childhood. Every child is known, met with patience, and woven into a community that cares for them.
